Science & Technology Issues Project Guide

Explore, analyze, and solve the most pressing science and technology issues of our time.

Objectives Concept Guide Solution Wall

Direction

Students will use inquiry skills to examine the science and technology issues that impact the contemporary world. Included in the project will be the analysis of the historical, cultural, economic, and geographic factors that have raised certain science and technology issues to levels of concern in our nation and around the globe. Students will engage in research and problem solving in order to better understand and assess significant current issues.

Objectives

Develop proficiency in using the skills and tools of inquiry
Analyze and understand the roles played by individuals, groups, and organizations on contemporary events and issues
Analyze and understand the relationship between history, geography, and politics and contemporary events and issues
Creatively propose a solution to contemporary issues in Science and Technology

Quick Rubric

  • Clarity: Is the problem and solution clearly described?
  • Evidence: Does the proposal reference credible sources?
  • Impact: Is the solution feasible and impactful?
  • Scope: Are cultural, geographic, economic, and political factors considered?
